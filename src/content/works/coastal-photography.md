## The Brief

A lifestyle magazine commissioned a photo series capturing the intersection of urban and coastal life in six port cities across Asia. The creative direction called for a moody, cinematic aesthetic with natural light only.

## The Result

42 photographs published across 12 pages. The series won a regional editorial photography award and was featured in three design publications. The magazine saw a 28% increase in issue sales for the feature edition.

## Behind the Scenes

Shot over 18 days using natural light and minimal equipment. Post-processing focused on color grading to create a consistent visual narrative across all six locations.
